    How It Works

    Step 1: Register Restaurant Branch

    Complete your restaurant registration online. Each restaurant branch pays a once-off registration fee of R2000.

    Step 2: Add Waitstaff

    Once the registration is complete our sales representatives will be in contact to create profiles for all your waitstaff to use. A monthly fee of R500 for 10 waitstaff is charged.

    Step 3: Add Menu

    Add all your menu products/meals to simplify table management for your waitstaff and always be able to update your menu to align with your restaurant or individual branch specials.
