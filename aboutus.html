    About Us

    At myTable, we are passionate about making restaurant operations smoother and more efficient. Our service is designed to empower waitstaff with modern tools that eliminate the stress of managing large groups, splitting bills, and tracking individual payments.

    Our Mission

    To simplify table management for waitstaff, creating a seamless dining experience for both staff and customers by removing the complexity of bill splitting and payment tracking.

    Our Vision

    To become the go-to solution for restaurants nationwide, fostering a positive environment where waitstaff confidently handle any table size with ease, ensuring customer satisfaction every time.

    Why We Created myTable

    The idea for myTable was born after witnessing the challenges waitstaff face when serving large groups. Calculating bills and splitting them among customers often leads to lengthy processes, unnecessary debates, and even mistakes. Many waitstaff struggle with or avoid large groups because of the difficulties associated with individual payment tracking systems. We created myTable to ease these challenges, allowing waitstaff to focus on what they do bestâ€”providing excellent service. By simplifying table and bill management, we aim to improve efficiency, reduce stress, and enhance the overall dining experience for everyone involved.
